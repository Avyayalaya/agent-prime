## Key Insight

These are not 6 independent documents -- each artifact feeds the next.

Problem Framing reveals that the real question is "which job should we own?" not "how do we beat Canva." Discovery Research surfaces the evidence (265M MAU, 8% conversion, 80-90% market share data) that makes competitive analysis falsifiable rather than speculative. Competitive Analysis identifies the only contestable space (vertical AI design), which Metric Design builds KPIs for. The Specification turns that opportunity into a zero-ambiguity build plan. The Narrative sells that specific product to the board.

Remove any single stage and the downstream artifacts lose their foundation.
